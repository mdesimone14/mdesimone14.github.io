Founded in Carol Stream, IL, The Information Nation: Chicago Sports Edition is a website built for all Chicago sports fans
looking for quick statistical information about the professional sports teams of Chicago. The teams include the Chicago Bears,
Chicago Bulls, Chicago Blackhawks, Chicago Cubs, Chicago White Sox and lastly, the Chicago Fire. We provide updated information
for each of the team's recent game scores, team news, injuries, divisonal and conference standings, win versus loss records,
as well as links to the current schedule and roster for each team.

Below is a list for the different services and products we offer:

  • Team Statistics
  • Team News
  • Injury Updates
  • Team Standings and Records
  • Merchandise
